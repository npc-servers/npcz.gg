SERVER RULES

Common Sense

Slurs

No use of homophobic and racial slurs are permitted. Homophobic & Racial slurs are strictly prohibited, no matter the context. Saying/promoting these terms will get you banned.

Harassment

Do not harass or intentionally annoy other players.

Mic Spam

Mic Spamming/earraping is not allowed.

English Only in All-Chat

We are an english speaking server, so keep all-chat in english. You can use other languages in PMs/Near/Squads.

Cheating/Exploiting

Modifying your game to provide an unfair advantage (cheating/exploiting) is against the rules.

Server Disruption

Intentionally lagging/crashing the server is not allowed.

Doxxing/DDoS

Threatening or attempting to dox/DDoS other players will result in a permanent ban.

Advertising

Advertising/recruiting in the server (Discord invites, clan recruiting, or server promotion) is not allowed.

NSFW Content

NSFW Content is not allowed.

Offensive Behavior

Do not engage in/use offensive behavior or language.

Rule Breaking Retaliation

Do not break rules because someone else is breaking them; you will also be punished.

Politics/Sensitive Topics

Topics about politics, shootings, ongoing wars, illegal content, etc, are highly discouraged, and will result in a punishment by staff.

Respect All Players

Treat everyone with respect. Excessive Harassment, hate speech, and personal attacks will not be tolerated.

Follow Admin Instructions

Always listen to the admins and moderators. Their word is final.

Prop Abuse

Griefing

Avoid behavior that disrupts the game experience for others, including excessive mic spam, punching & searching players without valid reason, or other forms of disruptive gameplay.

Prop Push/Kill

Do not prop push or prop kill under any circumstances.

Unauthorized Base Entry

Using props/vehicles to enter peoples bases is not allowed.

Prop Spam

Do not prop spam, doing this may result in a ban without warning!

Map Blocking

Blocking off large parts of the map, including roads and spawnpoints, is not allowed.

Skybox Building

Do not build in the skybox.

Unauthorized Props

Do not spawn props/entities in or on a player's build/base without permission. This includes spawnpoints.

Unauthorized Access Methods

Do not use props or balloons to gain access to a player's build/base. Propflying for map traversal is allowed.

PVE/PVP

RDM

Killing other players without a valid reason is prohibited. Roleplay interactions must be meaningful.

Teamkilling

Do not kill your teammates.

Revenge RDM

If you are RDM'd, do not attempt to RDM the player back. Report it to an admin.

Metagaming

Do not use knowledge gained from out-of-character sources (Private Voice-chats/Direct Messages) to influence your in-game decisions.

PVE Advantage

You may not use PVE mode to gain, or provide another player(s) any form of advantage in PVP.

Playermodel Restrictions

In PVP, equipping a smaller than average playermodel while in pvp mode is not allowed, same goes for playermodels that look normal but have smaller than usual hitboxes. If you are in PVE mode you are exempt from this rule.

Map Boundaries

Attacking players from outside the map or inside a space that isn't accessible is not allowed.

PVE Killing

While in PVE mode you are not allowed to kill other players in any way.

PVE Blocking

Do not use PVE mode to body block entries or areas.

PVE Base Respect

While in PVE mode, you must leave a PVP player's base if they ask you to.

Basing

Stay Inbounds

Do not abuse mechanics to get out of bounds on maps.

Keypad Access

In PVP, doors must have an easily accessible and visible keypad or button within close proximity for entering.

Keypad Restrictions

In PVP, you are not allowed to use keypads to disorient a raider (eg. multiple keypads for 1 door).

Prop Blocking

Using props to block off areas without keypads is not allowed. If you are in PVE mode you are exempt from this rule.

PVE Base Shooting

If you have a base in PVE you cannot let PVP players shoot out of it.

Base Accessibility

Bases must be completely enterable without crouching/obstruction.

Door Limits

Bases can have a maximum of 4 doors per entrance.

Wiremod/E2/Starfall/Contraptions

Exploiting/Cheating

Do not exploit bugs or glitches in the game. Report any issues to the server admins instead.

Malicious Intent

If you wouldn't fight it, don't use it! Anything built for malicious intent is not allowed.

Zombie Contraptions

Contraptions designed to automatically kill zombies in a large radius are not allowed. You are exempt from this rule if you are base building.

Contraption Health

If the intentions of the contraption is to fight with it you MUST have contraption health core present on the contraption. (see the construction tools section)

Contraption Power

Contraptions should be equal in power to their Glide counterparts.

Drone Restrictions

Drone contraptions are not allowed. You can use our counterparts in the entities tab or the !shop.

Player Targeting

Player targeting is not allowed. Using radars, Tracking Turrets, commands, or anything related is not allowed. This also includes player blinders and teleportation jails.

Optimization

Builds and contraptions must be optimized: paste without constraints, Prop2Mesh it, or rebuild it to be simpler. If your build degrades server or client performance, it will be deleted.

Failure to adhere to these rules may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans at the discretion of the admins. If you have doubts about saying or doing something, chances are that it's not allowed.
