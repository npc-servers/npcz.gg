How to Report a Player

Follow these simple steps to report a player who has violated our server rules. A proper report helps us take appropriate action quickly.

1

Copy the Player's SteamID

Right-click on the player's name in the scoreboard and select "Copy SteamID". If the player is offline, you can find their SteamID in:

  • The sandbox-relay channel in Discord
  • The chat log when they disconnected
2

Join Our Discord Server

Join our Discord server at discord.gg/npc. If you're not already a member, you'll need to accept the server rules first.

3

Navigate to #help Channel

Look for the #help channel near the top of the channels list on the left side of Discord.

4

Open the Report Form

Find and click the "Sandbox Support" panel, then select "Report a player on Sandbox" from the dropdown menu.

5

Fill Out the Report

Complete all required fields in the form:

  • What happened
  • Paste the SteamID you copied earlier
  • Type their username
  • Include any evidence you have, like a screenshot or video. This can also be done once the ticket is created via embed.
6

Provide Evidence

If you have any evidence (screenshots, recordings, chat logs), please attach them to your report. Evidence helps us:

  • Verify the incident
  • Take appropriate action quickly
  • Maintain a fair gaming environment
7

Check Your Report Thread

Your report will create a thread where staff will investigate your case. Remember to:

  • Check back on your report thread regularly
  • Respond to any questions from staff promptly
  • Provide additional information when requested
  • Keep the thread active until staff closes it

Result of Your Report

The result of your report will be sent to you by the NPCZ Support discord bot, where you can view the final outcome of your ticket, and its history.
